JEAN-GEORGES

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock two_col_description_1, type: text %}

Jean-Georges at Topping Rose House is a casually elegant restaurant that celebrates the seasons, presenting farm-to-table cuisine using ingredients grown on the property's one acre farm and sourced from the area's reputable fishermen and farmers. Open year-round for breakfast, lunch and dinner, Jean-Georges at Topping Rose House offers sensible fare paired with a contemporary Hamptons vibe, making it a welcoming restaurant for all ages, whether you’re looking to enjoy a simple bite or a polished dinner.
