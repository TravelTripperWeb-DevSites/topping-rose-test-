PLAN YOUR ARRIVAL TO KESWICK

{% endregionblock %}

DRIVING FROM WASHINGTON DC

Follow I-66 West to Exit 43A US-29 South. Follow US-29 South for 11.4 miles and merge left onto US-15 South/US-29 South. Follow US-15/US-29 for 26.5 miles and then take exit to US-15 South toward Culpepper/Orange. Follow US-15 South for 25.8 miles to VA-231 South. Follow VA-231 South for 9 miles and merge onto VA-22 West. Follow VA-22 West for 3.1 miles. Turn left onto Hunt Club Road and continue straight to Club Drive, Keswick Hall is on your left.

DRIVING FROM CHARLOTTESVILLE

Follow I-64 West for 57.1 miles to exit 129 for County Road toward Keswick. After 0.3 miles, turn right onto Black Cat Rd. After 0.3 miles on Black Cat Road, turn left on Club Drive and inform the gate you are a hotel guest. Follow Club Drive for 2 miles until you reach the 2 nd gate which will automatically open. Keswick Hall is on your right.

  • Alternate transportation: Yellow Cab of Charlottesville 434-295-4131
  • Estimated taxi fare: 30 USD (one way)

Charlottesville Airport

Driving directions:
Head west on State Rt. 606, at the traffic circle take the 2 nd exit. Continue to Earlysville road 3.8 miles. Turn right onto Hydraulic road for 2.2 miles. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto US-250 bypass continue US-250 east. Travel 6.4 miles then turn left onto VA 22 E for 1.6 miles. Turn right on Hunt Club Drive and follow to 701 Club Drive.

Richmond International Airport (RIC)
Airport Phone: +1 804-226-3000
Hotel distance: 73 miles
This hotel does not provide shuttle service.

  • Alternate transportation: Virginia Airlink; fee: 110 USD (one way)

Richmond Airport

Driving directions:
Travel 0.6 miles east on Federal Road/Richard E Byrd Terminal Drive and make a slight right onto South Airport Drive. Use the right lane to merge onto Interstate 64 West via the ramp to Interstate 95/Richmond. Travel 6.8 miles and keep right to stay on Interstate 64 West following signs for Interstate 95 North/Charlottesville/Washington. Travel 3.5 miles. Keep right at the fork in the road to stay on Interstate 64 West and follow signs for Interstate 64 West/Interstate 195 South/Powhite Parkway/Charlottesville. Travel 67.3 miles and take exit 129 County Rd. 616 toward Keswick, Boyd Tavern. Club Drive is 2.0 miles on the left.
